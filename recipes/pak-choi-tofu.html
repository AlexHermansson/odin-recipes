Pak Choi Tofu

Description

This very quick and simple dish is amazing. Some fried veggies on top of rice together with tofu.

Ingredients

  • 1 red bellpepper
  • 200g mushrooms (shiitake or oyster)
  • 2 heads of pak choi
  • 300g tofu
  • 2 dl rice
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • coconut aminos
  • sesame seeds
  • salt

Steps

  1. Chop the bellpepper and mushroom into slices, and the tofu into cubes
  2. Start the rice
  3. Put water to boil for the pak choi, and salt lightly
  4. Fry the bellpepper and mushrooms on medium heat in sesame oil for a few minutes
  5. Put them aside in a bowl and fry the tofu in the same pan
  6. Throw the pak choi into the boiling water for 1-2 minutes
  7. Serve rice with veggies and tofu. Put the pak choi on top, with some sesame seeds and coconut aminos sprinkled over.
