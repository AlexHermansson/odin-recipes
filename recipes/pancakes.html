Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe to make Swedish pancakes (the picture is a little misleading).

Ingredients

These are the ingredients for the pancakes:

  • 2 eggs
  • 4 dl oatmilk
  • 2 dl flour (gluten free is fine!)
  • a pinch of salt

Steps

Follow the steps below to make the pancakes:

  1. Whish the eggs.
  2. Add half of the oatmilk and whisk.
  3. Add the flour.
  4. Add the rest of the oatmilk, salt and whisk again.
  5. Heat up a pan on medium/high and add some oil
  6. Pour batter into 2/3 of the pan, then roll the pan to spread it
  7. Fry for 2-3 minutes on each side
